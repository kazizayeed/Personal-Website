Quick Notes

A collection of thoughts, ideas, and reflections.

  • August 02, 2025

    The Value of Starting Small

    Every large project feels intimidating at the start. The key is breaking it down into the smallest possible units of progress. Don't build a website; make a header. Don't write a paper; write a single paragraph. Small, consistent wins build momentum that giants can't stop.

  • July 28, 2025

    Feedback is a Tool, Not an Indictment

    It's easy to view criticism of your work as a criticism of yourself. I'm learning to reframe it. Feedback is just data. It's a tool provided by others to help you see the blind spots. Separate the information from the emotion, and you'll find it's one of the most valuable tools for growth.

  • July 21, 2025

    Leadership Isn't About Having the Answers

    True leadership isn't about being the smartest person in the room. It's about empowering others, asking the right questions, and creating an environment where the best ideas can surface, regardless of who they come from. It's about building a strong table, not just having the best seat at it.
